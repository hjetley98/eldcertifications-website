Exclusive Assessment

Regulator Readiness Index (RRI)

A Structured Compliance Risk Assessment for ELD Providers

The Regulator Readiness Index (RRI) is an independent, evidence-based assessment designed to evaluate your organization's preparedness for certification, audit, or regulatory review.

What the RRI Measures

01
Technical Conformance

Alignment of system behavior with applicable ELD technical standards

02
Test Case Coverage

Completeness and quality of required test case execution

03
Operational Consistency

Stability of workflows across normal, edge, and extended-duration scenarios

04
Documentation & Evidence

Audit-defensible documentation, traceability, and supporting materials

05
Regulatory Risk Exposure

Identification of gaps that may trigger audit findings or enforcement action

RRI Deliverables

  • Structured Readiness Score by category
  • Documented findings mapped to requirements
  • Identified areas of non-conformance
  • Prioritized remediation recommendations
  • Executive-level summary for stakeholders
Note: The RRI does not guarantee certification or approval and is provided solely to support internal compliance efforts.

Who the RRI Is For

  • ELD providers preparing for initial certification
  • Organizations responding to audit findings
  • Vendors recovering from revocation
  • Product teams seeking independent validation
  • Leadership requiring regulatory risk visibility
